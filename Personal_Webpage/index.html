Full Stack developer
I'm a Full Stack Web Developer based around Atlanta, GA. I attended Georgia Institute of Technology for my undergraduate degree where I majored in Industrial Engineering. After graduation I held a variety of positions from IT support to designing an assembly line in China. I soon discovered my passion for coding.
I quickly discovered similarities between the two process, but now instead of providing instructions for how many times for a worker to rotate a screw, I am now providing instructions for how many times for a computer to traverse a loop.
I decided I was ready for a new challenge and moved on to web development. Through analyzing online resources and lots of trial and error, I was able to create simple programs with simple functionality. To move on to the next step and to learn how to program professionally, I enrolled in DigitalCrafts' full-time full-stack immersive coding bootcamp in February of 2017. To see what I've been working on check out my portfolio!
